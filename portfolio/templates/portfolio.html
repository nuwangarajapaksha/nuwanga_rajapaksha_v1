My Self

Hello! I’m Minimuthu Rajapaksha Pathirannahalage Nuwanga Tharuksha Rajapaksha, a 24-year-old Software Engineer, Photographer, Graphic Designer and Video Editor from Kelaniya, Sri Lanka. I studied at Nalanda College Colombo 10, where my passion for technology, creativity, and leadership first began to grow. I earned a Bachelor’s degree in Software Engineering from Birmingham City University and now work professionally as a Software Engineer, developing innovative solutions to real-world challenges. While coding is my career, photography, graphic design and video editing are my creative outlets—hobbies that allow me to express emotion, capture beauty, and bring imagination to life in visual form.

Education

I began my education at Nalanda College, where I actively participated in extracurricular activities including working with the Computer Society. I passed the Ordinary Level (O/L) examination with excellent results. However, due to my high involvement in extracurricular responsibilities, I was unable to perform to my full potential in the Advanced Level (A/L) examination.

Driven by my long-standing passion for software development, I pursued professional qualifications awarded by the Scottish Qualifications Authority (SQA), completing both the Professional Diploma (SQAF Level 7), Professional Higher Diploma (SQAF Level 8), and PCJT Software Engineer III (SQAF Level 9) in Software Engineering. Eventually, I completed my Bachelor of Science with Honours in Software Engineering at Birmingham City University, UK, further strengthening my academic and technical foundation.

Profession

I have over two years of professional experience in full-stack development, having started as an intern and advanced to a Software Engineer at XdotO Concepts Pte Limited. In addition to my full-time role, I’ve pursued my passion through several freelance projects. My journey has taken me through a range of impactful projects, including an Industry 4.0 Machine Monitoring System, an Alarms and Notification Mobile Application, and a Process Monitoring System. These experiences have helped me build a strong foundation in modern development practices and architectural thinking, while also sharpening my skills in collaborative problem-solving, leadership, lifecycle management, client handling, teamwork, time management, communication, critical thinking, and nurturing a passion for continuous learning. I'm always eager to learn and stay up to date with the latest tools, frameworks, and industry trends.

Hobbies

Outside of my professional work, I’m deeply passionate about Photography—capturing landscapes, moments, and emotions with a creative eye. My photography is not only a form of artistic expression but also a source of income, as I actively sell my photos on stock platforms such as Shutterstock and Adobe Stock.

I also enjoy Graphic design and Video Editing, which allow me to explore visual storytelling and sharpen my creative skills. These hobbies directly contribute to my sense of aesthetics in UI/UX development. I have strong proficiency in the Adobe Creative Suite, including Photoshop, Illustrator, Premiere Pro, After Effects, and Lightroom.
