Commence

My passion for programming began during my school years at Nalanda College, Colombo 10, where I was first introduced to the fascinating world of computers. I studied ICT for both my Ordinary Level (O/L) and Advanced Level (A/L) examinations, achieving good marks in both. These studies gave me a strong foundation in various ICT concepts such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, MySQL, computer systems, software applications, networking, pseudo-code, algorithms, and the history of ICT. Through this learning, I developed a deep understanding of how websites and web applications are structured and styled, how logic and interactivity are implemented, how databases and networks operate, and how different components of a computer system function together. I also learned the fundamentals of programming logic, problem-solving using algorithms, and how to plan solutions using pseudocode. What truly ignited my enthusiasm was my active role in the Nalanda College Computer Society, where I organized tech events, participated in coding competitions, and mentored peers. This experience not only developed my technical skills but also helped me grow as a leader and team player, giving me the confidence and drive to pursue a professional career in software engineering.

Higher Studies

I earned a Bachelor of Science with Honours in Software Engineering from Birmingham City University, UK, through its affiliated delivery at the Java Institute for Advanced Technology, Sri Lanka. This internationally recognized degree equipped me with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills across a wide range of subjects, including Object-Oriented Programming Concepts, Object-Oriented Design Patterns, Data Structures and Algorithms, Object-Oriented System Analysis and Design, Cyber Law, Web Development, Software Development, Computer Hardware, Networking, Database Management Systems, Electronics for Software Engineering, and Handheld Device Programming. I also worked extensively with languages and tools such as Java, Java EE, Java Swing, PHP, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Android, Hibernate, JPA, JUnit MySQL and Firebase. Through practical assignments, lab sessions, group projects, and real-world simulations, I was able to apply what I learned in the classroom to build functional applications and solve complex problems, which significantly enhanced my technical and analytical skills. As part of my university coursework, I also developed several fully functional e-commerce applications, m-commerce applications, and desktop applications that demonstrated my ability to apply theoretical knowledge to real-world software solutions. Prior to the degree, I completed a structured progression of qualifications awarded by the Scottish Qualifications Authority (SQA)— the Professional Diploma in Software Engineering (SQAF Level 7), the Professional Higher Diploma in Software Engineering (SQAF Level 8), and the PCJT Software Engineer III (SQAF Level 9)—which laid a solid foundation for my academic and professional success in the software engineering field.

Career

Professionally, I have gained two years of professional experience as a full-stack developer at XdotO Concepts Pte Limited, having joined as an intern and quickly advanced to a Software Engineer role, developing robust skills in both backend and frontend technologies. During my time there, I contributed to several key projects, including Melioraa (an Industry 4.0 Machine Monitoring System), Dialog Power App (an Alarms and Notification Mobile Application), and Melioraa (a Process Monitoring System). I designed and developed scalable web applications using Java, Spring Boot, ReactJS, VueJS, PHP, GO, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, Material-UI, Grafana, JUnit, MySQL, Hibernate, JPA, Axios, JSON, and Jasper Reports. I built and maintained RESTful APIs, worked with microservices architecture, and implemented micro-frontend solutions. My responsibilities also included Dockerizing applications, managing CI/CD pipelines, and handling deployments with AWS. I worked in Agile teams, used Jira for tracking, Git(GitHub, GitLab) for version control, and Postman/Swagger for API testing and documentation. I also developed leadership skills by leading small teams, handling planning, delegation, and reviews. This role enhanced my communication, problem-solving, and collaboration capabilities.

In addition to my full-time role, I have passionately pursued freelance projects that allowed me to explore and apply a wide range of technologies beyond the scope of my main job. Driven by curiosity and a love for building meaningful solutions, I took on several freelance assignments where I independently learned and implemented Python, Django, FastAPI, and MongoDB. These projects not only sharpened my technical skills but also deepened my problem-solving abilities, strengthened my self-discipline, and fueled my continuous learning mindset—turning my passion into tangible results.
